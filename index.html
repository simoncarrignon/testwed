Ski resort in winter, in the summer take the cable car up to the top to enjoy stunning views of the Alpes and Grenoble city Official website

Charavines Lake

Beautiful lake on the way to Lyon airport with a neolithic settlement (in Charavine's village)

Vizille

Historic castle and gardens, where the French Revolution started.

Grenoble Bastille

Cable car gives you views over the city, especially nice at night.
